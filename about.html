About Me

Welcome to my blog! I am a passionate Security Engineer with a deep commitment to safeguarding digital environments. My name is Guftar Raza, and I hold a degree in Software Engineering from NUST, Pakistan. With over two years of hands-on experience (Since:2023) in cloud security, primarily focusing on the Azure Security Stack, I am dedicated to implementing innovative and effective security solutions.

My journey in the field of cybersecurity began with a strong foundation in software engineering, which has equipped me with a unique perspective on the integration of secure practices within development processes. I specialize in leveraging Azure’s suite of security tools to create resilient defenses against emerging threats, ensuring that businesses and their data remain protected.

On this blog, I share insights, best practices, and updates related to cloud security, as well as tips and tricks for navigating the complex landscape of digital protection. Whether you are a fellow security professional or someone interested in learning more about securing your digital assets, I hope you find valuable information here.
